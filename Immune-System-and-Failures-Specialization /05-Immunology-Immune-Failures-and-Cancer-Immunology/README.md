## ğŸ“š Course Overview  

This course examines the **failures of the human immune system**, including **immunodeficiencies, chronic inflammation, and immune dysregulation in cancer**.  
Learners explore the mechanisms leading to insufficient or overactive immune responses and their consequences for **disease susceptibility, tissue damage, and tumour development**.  

The course also addresses **tertiary lymphoid structures**, **tumour-immune interactions**, and the role of **viral infections in human cancer**, providing a comprehensive view of immune system dysfunction and therapeutic strategies.
