## ğŸ“š Course Overview  

This course explores the constant **interplay between pathogens and the human immune system**, highlighting the mechanisms of defense and the strategies pathogens use to evade immunity.  
It examines **bacteria, viruses, fungi, parasites, and prions**, their interactions with host immunity, and the consequences of immune evasion, including disease development.

Learners gain knowledge on how **molecular and cellular immune responses** protect the host, and how pathogens exploit **immune evasion strategies**, sometimes resulting in severe infections.
