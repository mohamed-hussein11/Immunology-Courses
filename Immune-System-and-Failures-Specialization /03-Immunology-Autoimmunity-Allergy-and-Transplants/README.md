## ğŸ“š Course Overview  

This course explores the **mechanisms and immune responses** involved in autoimmune diseases and hyper-responsive immune conditions such as **asthma**, focusing on the consequences of an **overactive immune system**.  
It examines the **causes, risk factors, immune dysregulation, and pathological outcomes** underlying autoimmunity, allergies, and transplant rejection.

Learners analyze how disrupted immune regulation leads to:  
- **Self-aggression and autoimmune disease**  
- **Haematological autoimmune disorders**  
- **Asthma and allergic hypersensitivity reactions**  
- **Antibody-mediated graft rejection**  

The course combines mechanistic understanding with clinical relevance and modern treatment strategies.
